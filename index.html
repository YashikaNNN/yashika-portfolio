Who I Am

A little bit about me

Hi, I'm Yashika — a developer by training, a data enthusiast by choice, and someone who has a very complicated relationship with bugs (they find me, always). I spent a year building full-stack web applications professionally and debugging them, which quietly taught me that understanding data is the real superpower behind every good application. So here I am, learning it properly.

I'm currently pursuing my MCA in AI & Data Science at Graphic Era University — turning chaotic data into something that actually makes sense, one dataset at a time.

Outside of screens, I play guitar — badly enough that my neighbors have opinions, passionately enough that I don't care. I practice yoga and meditation daily, which is the universe's way of balancing out how chaotic my git commits look.

Fun facts: My commit messages follow a very clear arc: "initial setup" → "fix" → "fix again" → "why" → "PLEASE WORK" → "finally." It's not a bug, it's a process.
